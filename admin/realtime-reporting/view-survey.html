Part I: Referral / Incentive Scheme

Incentive Scheme is a process of student informing other students to answer the survey, and in return, the other students will be able to select the one informing under a “Referral” below.

Each time a student name is selected under referral, it will contribute as point which will be accumulated within a survey timeframe. Winners will be selected at the end of the survey timeframe and Taylor's will distribute gifts to winners. All points will be refreshed each survey year.

If you do not wish to participate on the Incentive Scheme, you may choose "Not Applicable" from the dropdown.

You only can select ONE referral name. Once this survey is submitted, it cannot be changed in the future.
